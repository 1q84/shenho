公司简介

紳豪公司成立于2008年，位在中國浙江省溫州市，主要經營國際貿易業務，在于董事長的代領及公司全体人員的不斷努力下，公司年有成長，因應業務的需要和企業的進展，公司在2012年8月，于香港九龍成立了分公司，主要經營橡膠，大米，農業產品等項目，在中國，泰國，加拿大，緬甸，越南，高棉，老撾等國，有著穩定成長的貿易業績!

Shenhao Company was founded in 2008 which lies in Wenzhou City of Zhejiang Province in the People Republic of China. It mainly engaged in international trade business, and the company has grown year by year with the lead of CEO as well as the effort of staff. Due to the needs of business and the development of the enterprise, branch office was founded in August, 2012 in Kowloon, Hong Kong. This branch office mainly engaged in rubber, rice, agricultural products and other items, which achieves a stable growth of trade performance in China, Thailand, Canada, Myanmar, Vietnam, Cambodia, Lao People's Democratic Republic and other related countries.
